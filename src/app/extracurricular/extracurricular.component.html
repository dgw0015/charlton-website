Social Sorority

Even though I grew up in Auburn, attending the University was a much difference experience. Most of my friends went to different schools so I decided I needed to branch out and meet new people. I knew that joining a sorority would serve as a great avenue to meet new people and form new connections. While in my sorority I served as the Assistant to Alumnae Relations Chair. In this position, I attended biweekly meetings to discuss the various upcoming events for the organization and assisted in planning and executing events involving alumnae and current members and their families. From my time in the sorority, I have met so many amazing women with so many different backgrounds giving me a new perspective on people and has allowed me to develop priceless friendships in the process.
