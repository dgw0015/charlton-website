Writing Samples

Below you will find a few examples of my projects that I created for the Hospitality Catering and Event Management class that was in charge of planning the Hospitality Gala 2020. Through these projects I have learned so much about communication and presentation. The writing samples include my color scheme inspiration document that was chosen as the Gala 2020 color scheme. Further, it includes a centerpiece assignment that required the team to find inspiration for our arrangements and quotes from different vendors. Lastly, you will see a link to the MoodBoard that the team created to present to the leadership team in hopes that it will be incorporated into the Gala for 2020.
